Welcome to Smart Plug Switcher

Imagine if your home appliances could automatically adjust their schedules to use energy when it’s at its greenest. With Smart Plug Switcher, you can effortlessly set your devices—like your Electric (water) Heaters, Home Battery systems, Hot Tubs, Fans, etc.—to run during peak solar or wind hours. This means lower energy bills, less strain on the power grid, and a smaller carbon footprint.

To get started, all you need is a standard smart plug and an internet connection.

Recommended Devices

  • Home Battery Systems
  • Electric Vehicle Chargers
